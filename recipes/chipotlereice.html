Copycat Chipotle Rice Side Dish

This copycat Chipotle rice, with lemon, lime, and cilantro, is easy to make at home, and tastes just like the restaurant version.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 1/2 cups white rice
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped cilantro

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a medium saucepan over medium high heat. Add rice and salt and cook, stirring constantly, about 1 minute.

  2. Add water and bay leaf and stir once. Bring to a boil, cover, and reduce heat to a low simmer.

  3. Continue cooking until liquid is absorbed and rice is tender, about 15 minutes.

  4. Remove from heat and fluff with a fork. Add lemon juice, lime juice and cilantro and stir with a fork until combined.

  5. Serve and enjoy!
